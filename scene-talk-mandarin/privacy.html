Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 18, 2026

SceneTalk Mandarin ("the App") respects your privacy. The App is designed for offline Mandarin learning and does not require an account or login.

1. Personal Information

The App does not collect, store, upload, share, or sell any personal information.

2. Local App Data

The App may save learning-related data locally on your device, such as:

  • language and display settings
  • playback preferences
  • learning progress
  • favorites or review status

This data stays on your device. It is not uploaded to the developer or to any developer-operated server.

3. In-App Purchases

The App may offer App Store in-app purchases to unlock categories, scenes, or full access. Purchase transactions are processed by Apple. The developer does not receive your payment card details or Apple account password.

4. Offline Use

The App can be used offline. Learning content, text, translations, and audio are bundled with the App or stored locally on the device.

5. Advertising and Tracking

The App does not contain ads, advertising SDKs, third-party analytics, or third-party tracking services.

6. Children’s Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7. Your Choices

You may delete the App at any time to remove locally stored app data from your device.

8. Contact

If you have any privacy-related questions, please contact: support@quxionglie.com

隐私政策

最后更新日期：2026年5月18日

SceneTalk Mandarin（以下简称“本应用”）重视你的隐私。本应用用于离线中文场景学习，不需要注册账号或登录。

1. 个人信息

本应用不会收集、存储、上传、共享或出售任何用户个人信息。

2. 本地应用数据

本应用可能会在你的设备本地保存与学习体验相关的数据，例如：

  • 语言和显示设置
  • 播放偏好
  • 学习进度
  • 收藏或复习状态

这些数据仅保存在你的设备上，不会上传到开发者或开发者运营的服务器。

本应用可能提供 App Store 应用内购买，用于解锁分类、场景或完整访问功能。购买交易由 Apple 处理，开发者不会获取你的支付卡信息或 Apple 账户密码。

4. 离线使用

本应用可离线使用。学习内容、文本、翻译和音频随应用内置或保存在设备本地。

5. 广告与追踪

本应用没有广告，不接入广告 SDK，不使用第三方统计分析服务，也不使用第三方追踪服务。

7. 你的选择

你可以随时删除本应用，以移除保存在本地设备中的应用数据。

8. 联系方式

SceneTalk Mandarin Support

SceneTalk Mandarin Support

SceneTalk Mandarin is an iOS app for learning practical Mandarin through real-life scenes. The app includes scenario-based dialogues, reusable expressions, listening practice, cultural notes, and background articles to help learners speak Chinese in everyday situations.

Contact

For support inquiries, please contact:

  • Email: support@quxionglie.com

App Introduction

English

SceneTalk Mandarin
Subtitle: Speak Chinese in Real Life

SceneTalk Mandarin helps Mandarin learners practice useful Chinese for real-life situations such as greetings, asking for directions, taking transportation, ordering food, shopping, and daily communication. Each scene focuses on practical dialogue, key expressions, listening practice, and cultural context.

The app does not require an account or login. It does not contain ads, and it does not collect, upload, store, or sell personal information. Learning content is available offline.

简体中文

SceneTalk Mandarin
副标题： Speak Chinese in Real Life

SceneTalk Mandarin 是一款面向中文学习者的 iOS 应用，帮助用户在真实生活场景中学习和练习中文。应用内容覆盖打招呼、问路、交通出行、点餐、购物和日常沟通等场景，每个场景包含实用对话、核心表达、听力练习和中文文化语境说明。

本应用不需要注册账号或登录。应用没有广告，不会收集、上传、存储或出售任何用户个人信息。学习内容可离线使用。

Privacy Summary

  • No account or login is required.
  • No advertising is included.
  • No personal information is collected, uploaded, stored, or sold.
  • Learning progress and settings remain on the user’s device.
  • The app can be used offline.
